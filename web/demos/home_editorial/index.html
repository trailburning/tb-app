Make your own Trail Cards

Capture every trail you run, race you’re in and mountain you climb. Transform the locations and photos into your own folio of Trail Cards. The Trail Maker will be as simple as Record, Share, and Inspire.

Record

Capture trails with a GPS enabled device and a camera.

Share

Make a Trail Card and present your experience.

Inspire

Be an inspiration and get inspired by other trailers.
