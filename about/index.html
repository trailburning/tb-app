What are we doing?

Trailburning wants everyone to enjoy the world's trails.

Trailburning.com is dedicated to presenting and sharing the world's trails as a beautiful 'play back' experience. There is a focus on trails for runners to discover places to run in new locations, however Trailburning is for everyone interested in trails – hikers, mountain bikers, skiers and walkers. The site will feature a growing list of trails from every region of the world, including major events and local races, and in the future we will launch ways to make it easy for our community member's to create personalised trail experiences to share.

Discover and share Trail Cards

Trail Cards are unique trail experiences.

We wanted to make it easy to discover trails so we created Trail Cards. Each card is an individual trail that presents a unique trail experience from the perspective of a community member or the Trailburning crew. A Trail Card links to a trail player that jumps you into a photographic story of a fellow trailer's run (or hike, walk, etc), and it's the best way to visualise trails on a map.

Coming soon is the ability to create your own Trail Cards, trailmaker™ will be used by all Trailburning members to make and share their personalised trails. It's as simple as recording a trail with a GPS enabled device and capturing photos along the way – then Trailburning combines the trail map with your photographs.
