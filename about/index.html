What are we doing?

Trailburning has been created to show the world amazing trails.

Trailburning.com was made for the trail running community to be able to share trail experiences. However, Trailburning can be utilised by anyone interested in trails – hikers, mountain bikers, skiers and walkers. The site will feature our community member's personalised trails, and a growing list of events and races.

Discover and share Trail Cards

Trail Cards are individual trails that can be shared.

After recording a trail with a GPS enabled device and capturing photos along the way you can can use Trailburning to unite your trail map with your photographs.

Coming soon is a trail creation tool that can be used by all Trailburning members to make their personalised trails.
