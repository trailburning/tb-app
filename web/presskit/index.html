Trailburning will be breaking new ground (and trails) in 2014.

Trailburning is a site for runners (and hikers) to discover new trails and the Trailburning.com beta site is a sneak preview for what we are launching in 2014.

Trailburning.com has been created by founder Matt Allbeury (Berlin, Germany) and co-founder Justin Wilden (Melbourne, Australia). They got together in April 2013 to start 'something' that will redefine the way we interact with geolocation media. Matt is the CEO and technologist driving the vision for Trailburning, and Justin is the company's resident strategist and marketer.

Please view the press release below to read the story behind Trailburning.com, and feel free to use the images below in editorial or blogs (please link back to our site).
