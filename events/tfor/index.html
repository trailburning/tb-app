Thames Festival of Running

A weekend celebration of “everything running” centered on the superb, self-contained ashmei race village.

This race village sits in a beautiful rural lakeside location, within 50 metres of the River Thames. At the heart of the race village is a luxury, weather-proof tensioned canopy tent. This magnificent structure has a capacity of 500 people. Within it will be a fire pit, lounge seating, chill-out zone and a dining space. There will be nightly film screenings and a DJ mixing smooth grooves on Saturday night.
